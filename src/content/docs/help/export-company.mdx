Exporting your company data creates a downloadable ZIP package containing all company data and associated files. This is primarily used to migrate a company from one Operately installation to another or from the cloud version to a self-hosted installation (or vice versa).

## Who can export company data

Only company owners can export company data.

## How to export your company data

<Steps>

1. Click on your company name in the top navigation bar to open the company menu.

2. Select **Company Admin** from the dropdown menu.

3. Click **Export company data** in the list of admin actions.

4. Click the **Start export** button.

5. Wait for the export job to finish. A progress bar and percentage indicator show the current status.

6. Once the status changes to **completed**, click **Download** to save the ZIP package to your computer.

</Steps>

## Things to know

- Export runs asynchronously in the background — you can navigate away and return to the Export page to check progress.
- The Export page shows a history of all export runs with their date and status.
- To use the exported ZIP to set up a company on another Operately instance, see [Import company data](/help/import-company).

## Version compatibility

Importing an export package into a different Operately version may fail. Keep the following in mind:

- **Self-hosted to self-hosted:** both the source and destination installations should run the same Operately version before exporting and importing.
- **Cloud to self-hosted (or vice versa):** the Operately cloud always runs the latest version. Make sure your self-hosted installation is updated to the latest version before performing the migration. See [Update a self-hosted installation](/help/self-hosted-update).
