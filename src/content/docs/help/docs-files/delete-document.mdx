---
title: "Delete a Document"
description: "Remove a document from your space."
---

import { Aside, Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

Anyone in the space who has [edit permissions](/help/space-access-control) can delete a document.

To delete a document:

<Steps>
1. Open the document you want to delete.
2. Click on the **...** menu in the top right corner.
3. Select **Delete** from the dropdown menu.
</Steps>

<Aside>Deleted documents cannot be recovered.</Aside>
