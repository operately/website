description: "Start a conversation about a document, or reply to existing comments."
---

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

You can discuss a document by starting a conversation or replying to existing
comments. When you discuss a document, you can share feedback, ask questions,
or provide additional context.

To discuss a document:

<Steps>
1. Open the document you want to discuss.
2. Click in the comment box at the bottom of the document that says "Write a comment here..."
3. Type your comment.
4. Click **Post** to add your comment to the document.
</Steps>

People who are subscribed to the document will be notified of new comments. You
can also mention specific people in your comments to notify them directly.

#### Edit your comments

You can edit your comments after you post them. 

To edit a comment:

<Steps>
1. Click on the **...** menu next to your comment.
2. Select **Edit** from the dropdown menu.
3. Make your changes to the comment.
4. Click **Save** to save your changes.
</Steps>

#### Delete your comments

You can delete your comments after you post them.

To delete a comment:

<Steps>
1. Click on the **...** menu next to your comment.
2. Select **Delete** from the dropdown menu.
</Steps>
