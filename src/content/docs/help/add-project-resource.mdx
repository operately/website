Resources are links to external documents, tools, or websites related to your project. Add a URL and title to create a simple list that appears at the bottom of the Overview tab. For example, design mockups in Figma, technical specifications in Google Drive, or Slack chat room.

## How to add a resource

<Steps>
1. Open the project page and go to the **Overview** tab
2. Click **Add resource**
3. Enter the URL of the resource
4. Enter a title for your resource
6. Click **Save**
</Steps>

## Managing resources

Once created, you can:

- **Edit** them to update titles or links
- **Delete** resources that are no longer needed
- **Share links** to specific resources with team members
