To remove someone's membership from your [space](/help/intro-to-spaces):

<Steps>
1. Go to your space
2. Click the **Manage access** button next to the member avatars
3. Find the person in the **Members** section
4. Click the menu (•••) next to their name
5. Select **Remove from space**
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Only space managers with Full Access can remove members.
</Aside>
