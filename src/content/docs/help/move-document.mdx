description: "Organize your documents by moving them to a different folder."
---

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

You can move documents to different folders to organize your content.

To move a document:

<Steps>
1. Open the document you want to move.
2. Click on the "..." menu in the top right corner.
3. Select **Move** from the dropdown menu.
4. Choose the folder where you want to move the document.
5. Click **Move** to move the document.
</Steps>

Members of the space will be notified that the document was moved.
