Operately gives you control over how and when you receive email notifications. All settings are in one place: the **Notification Settings** page.

## How to open notification settings

<Steps>
1. Click on your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. Click **Notification Settings**.
</Steps>

## Available settings

**[Activity email delivery](/help/activity-email-delivery)** — Choose whether all activity emails are batched together or whether direct mentions are sent to you right away. You can also set how long Operately waits before sending batched emails.

**[Daily summary email](/help/daily-summary-email)** — Enable a single end-of-day summary email and choose what time it is delivered.

**[Assignments email](/help/assignments-email)** — Enable or disable a daily email listing your upcoming check-ins, reviews, and other work that needs your attention.
