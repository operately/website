You can review which AI clients can access Operately through MCP and revoke a connection when you no longer want a client to use your account.

## Revoke a connection

<Steps>
1. Open **Account** → **Password & Security**.
2. Select **MCP Connections**.
3. Under **Connected Clients**, find the client you want to manage.
4. Open its options menu and click **Revoke**.
5. Confirm by clicking **Revoke**.
</Steps>

The page shows each client's access, when it connected, and when it was last used. After you revoke a connection, the client must reconnect through OAuth before it can access Operately again.
