To promote someone to be a manager of your [space](/help/intro-to-spaces):

<Steps>
1. Go to your space
2. Click the **Manage access** button next to the member avatars
3. Find the person in the **Members** section
4. Click the menu (•••) next to their name
5. Select **Promote to manager**
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Only space managers with Full Access can promote other members to managers.
</Aside>

<Aside type="tip">
Space managers automatically get Full Access to the space. They can add or remove members and change access levels. See [Space Access Control](/help/space-access-control) for more information.
</Aside>
