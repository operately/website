To add one or more people as members of your [space](/help/intro-to-spaces):

<Steps>
1. Go to your space
2. Click the **Manage access** button next to the member avatars
3. Click **Add Members** in the top right
4. In the "Member" field, start typing a person's name
5. Select the person from the dropdown
6. Choose their [access level](/help/space-access-control)
7. To add more people at once:
   - Click the **+** button below the form
   - A new row appears where you can add another person
   - Repeat steps 4-6 for each person
8. Click **Add members** when you're done
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Only space managers with Full Access can add new members.
</Aside>
