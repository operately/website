Keeping your profile information up-to-date in Operately helps your team identify you, ensures timestamps are displayed correctly, and populates the organization chart accurately.

## Accessing your profile

<Steps>
1. Click on your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.
</Steps>

## Updating your profile information

On the **Profile** page, you can update the following:

- **Name:** Enter your full name as you'd like it to appear throughout Operately.
- **Title in Company:** Enter your job title.
- **Timezone:** Select your local timezone from the dropdown menu.
- **Time format:** Choose how times are displayed:
  - **Automatic**
  - **12-hour clock**
  - **24-hour clock**
- **Who is your manager?:**
    - Select **No manager** if applicable.
    - Select **Select manager** to choose your direct manager from the list of team members. This information is used to build the organization chart. Keeping this updated ensures the [company org chart](/help/view-company-org-chart) is accurate.

<Aside>
**Timezone** controls which local time is shown. **Time format** controls how the time is displayed (12-hour vs 24-hour).
</Aside>

## Saving your changes

After making your desired updates, click the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the page.

Your profile information will be updated across Operately.
