import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

Anyone in a space can create a new document.

To create a new document:

<Steps>
1. Open the space where you want to create the document.
2. Click on the **Documents & Files** tool.
2. Click the **Add** button in the top left corner.
3. Select **New Document**.
4. Give your document a name and add your content.
5. Click **Submit** to save your document.
</Steps>

#### Notifying people about the new document

Before you submit the document, you can choose to notify people in the 
space about the new document. By default, everyone in the space will be
notified about the new document. 

You can choose to notify specific people in the space by selecting 
**Only these people I select** and then selecting the people you want 
to notify.

Alternatively, you can choose **No one** to not notify anyone about the
new document.
