The daily summary sends one email at the end of your workday that collects all activity from that day.

## How to enable/disable the daily summary

<Steps>
1. Click on your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. Click **Notification Settings**.
4. Under **Daily summary**, toggle the switch to **On** or **Off**.
5. Choose your preferred **Delivery time** from the dropdown.
6. Click **Save Changes**.
</Steps>

<Aside>The daily summary is off by default.</Aside>

## Choosing a delivery time

Delivery time can be set to any hour of the day. Choose a time that fits the end of your typical workday so the summary arrives when you're wrapping up.
