To change what everyone in your company can do in your [space](/help/intro-to-spaces):

<Steps>
1. Go to your space
2. Click the **Manage access** button next to the member avatars
3. Find the **General Access** section
4. Click **Edit** next to "Company-wide Access"
5. Choose the access level you want to give to everyone in your company
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Only space managers with Full Access can change company-wide access settings.
</Aside>
