Operately batches activity emails to reduce noise. You can choose whether direct mentions should also be batched or arrive right away, and how long the batch window lasts.

## Delivery mode

There are two options:

**Batched notifications** — All activity emails wait for the buffer window, are grouped together, and sent as a single email. This is the default.

**Direct mentions are instant** — Emails for direct mentions are sent right away. All other activity emails still wait for the buffer window and are sent together.

### How to change your delivery mode

<Steps>
1. Click on your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. Click **Notification Settings**.
4. Under **Activity emails**, select your preferred option.
5. Click **Save Changes**.
</Steps>

## Buffer window

The buffer window controls how long Operately waits before sending batched emails. A shorter window means more frequent emails; a longer window groups more activity into each email.

Available options: **5**, **10**, **15**, **30**, or **60 minutes**.

The buffer window applies to all batched emails — including activity emails in "Direct mentions are instant" mode.
