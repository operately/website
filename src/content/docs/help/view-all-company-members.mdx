Operately provides a central directory where you can see everyone who is part of your organization.

## How to access the People directory

<Steps>
1.  Click on your organization name in the top navigation bar to open the company menu.
2.  Select **People** from the dropdown menu.
</Steps>

## What you'll find

The People page displays a list of all active members in your organization. For each member, you can typically see:

*   Their profile picture or initials
*   Their full name
*   Their job title or role

You can click on any person's name or picture to navigate directly to their detailed [profile page](/help/view-person-profile).
