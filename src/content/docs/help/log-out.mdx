title: Log out of Operately
description: Learn how to securely log out of your Operately account.
---

import { Steps } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

When you're finished using Operately, it's good practice to log out to keep your account secure, especially if you're using a shared computer.

How to log out:

<Steps>
1. Click on your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. This will open the **My Account** menu.
3. Click the **Sign Out** button at the bottom of the menu.
</Steps>

You will be immediately logged out and redirected to the Operately log in screen.
