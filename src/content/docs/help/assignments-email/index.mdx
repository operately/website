The assignments email is a daily digest that lists your upcoming check-ins, reviews, and other work that needs your attention. It is separate from activity notifications and arrives once per day.

## How to enable/disable the assignments email

<Steps>
1. Click on your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of the screen.
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. Click **Notification Settings**.
4. Under **Assignments email**, toggle the switch to **On** or **Off**.
5. Click **Save Changes**.
</Steps>

<Aside>The assignments email is on by default. Disable it if you prefer not to receive it.</Aside>
